Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Mc Electronics And Electrics ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details (phone number, email address), and payment information when you purchase products or services from us. We also collect data related to your device repair history or recharge transactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide our services, process transactions, communicate with you regarding your orders or repairs, and improve our customer service. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
